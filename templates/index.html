What is PromptLens

Prompting is a skill.
Most people don't know they're bad at it.

When you write a prompt and get a bad output — you change a word, try again, hope for the best. There's no feedback. No score. No way to know if you're actually improving.

PromptLens fixes that. It scores your prompts, tells you exactly what's wrong, and shows you the difference between versions — so you learn by doing, not by guessing.
